Frequently asked questions

Do I need to book in advance?
Yes, we recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to secure your preferred time slot. We do accept walk‑ins when availability allows, but priority is given to booked appointments.

What should I expect during my first visit?
Your first visit includes a comprehensive skin consultation, during which we assess your skin type, discuss your goals, and tailor a treatment plan just for you. We also provide after‑care advice to maximise your results.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
Absolutely. We ask for at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may incur a fee, but we understand that emergencies happen — just give us a call.
